## Get in touch

Have a question, suggestion, or need help with TARMAC? We're here to help. Before reaching out, check if your answer is already covered in our documentation.

## Office Hours

The TARMAC team hosts weekly office hours every Thursday from 3:00 PM to 4:00 PM IST. Drop in to:

- Get live help with implementation questions
- Review your designs with the team
- Discuss upcoming features and roadmap
- Share feedback and suggestions

<Info>
  Office hours are open to all designers and developers using TARMAC. No appointment needed.
</Info>
